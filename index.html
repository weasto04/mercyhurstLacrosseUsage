About this app:
This interactive tool visualizes Mercyhurst lacrosse player data for the selected season. Each point represents a player (x = shots taken, y = goals scored).
Enter the number of shots you expect to take and choose k to see a k-nearest neighbor regression prediction for goals scored, based on the most similar players. Orange points are the k closest players (by shots), and the red point shows your predicted goals. Hover any point for details.
Data source: mercyhurst_lacrosse.csv (loaded locally)
